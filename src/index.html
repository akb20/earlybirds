About Us

As a professional international education agency, Earlybirds has been providing global courses and teachers for promary schools, high schools. and universities since 2011, reacging about ten thousand students annually.


Our specialties include creative course development, quality control, teacher training, and building international camp culture.

Our Academic Partners

Earlybirds strives to offer tailored solutions to each of the academic institutions we partner with. We have a history of success working within a variety of educational institutions at the University level.


Our goal when working with our partner institutions is to provide academic solutions that are both innovative and flexible. We pride ourselves on the long-term relationships we have built up with our current partners and see this as a strategic aim of our organization when entering into new partnerships.
