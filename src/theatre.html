Youth theatre is for students with a passion and talent for stage performing and outstanding English speaking ability. We provide them with a chance to learn and act with professional actors and directors. Working in a professional environment will give students a real sense of achievement and personal growth. Highlights include project based learning, audition competition, professional performance experience, charity and community contribution.

Earlybirds also funds troupes to present family plays to local and expat communities in Shanghai with international standards. The shows Earlybirds has produced cast both professional adult actors and selected kids actors. We aim to produce family plays that are memorable for both kids and adults.

In 2017, Earlybirds brought The Jungle Book on professional stage in Shanghai. Back by popular demand, The Jungle Book returns with a bigger cast (7 professional adult actors plus 22 child actors, most of them new), new songs and dance in 2018.

We look forward to bring more great shows to a greater audience!

If you're interested in learning more about upcoming events, or would like to arrange a demo class, feel free to get in touch with us.
